HOW IT WORKS

SEND YOUR FOOTAGE

Just take the footage out of your camera and send it over to us. You can use any cloud storage of your choice but suggest either Dropbox or Google Drive. Oh, and one more thing. When I said we can take footage from any kind of device we really mean it. So you can send videos straight from you iPhone, GoPro or DSLR camera.

WE EDIT IT

Once all your footage is uploaded, the project will be assigned to a Video Editing Engineer which is specialized in working on that particular type ofproject.Over at Fireside Studios we have dedicated professionals for projects in different niches such as Wedding, GoPro, Drone, Corporate or Family video..The average turnaround time for a standard project is 7 working days.

REVIEW & PAY

Once we are done editing, you will hear back from us and get a draft copy.
After reviewing the draft copy, you can ask for one free revision.

DOWNLOAD YOUR VIDEO

Finally you get to enjoy a nicely crafted video which you can share with
your friends & family, YouTube subscribers or perhaps with your customers.
